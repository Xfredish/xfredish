About us

At YOU AND ME CLEANING SERVICES, our story is rooted in family, hard work, and a shared passion for creating clean, inviting spaces. Founded by a dedicated father-son duo, we believe that a clean environment is not just about aesthetics—it's about fostering a sense of well-being and community. Our journey began in our own neighborhood, where we saw a need for reliable, high-quality cleaning services. With a commitment to excellence and a strong desire to make a difference, we decided to turn our shared dream into reality.
